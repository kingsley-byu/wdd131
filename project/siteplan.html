MealDash - Site Plan

Online Food Ordering Website. Site plan Document

MealDash

This name represents a club that is focused on online food ordering in the San Francisco Bay Area

Site purpose

MealDash is a responsive, front-end online food ordering website where users can browse
a mixed menu of local and international dishes, add items to a cart, and submit an order.
The site simulates a real ordering experience using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

Scenarios

Everyone — students, families, professionals, and anyone who enjoys a wide variety of food options
